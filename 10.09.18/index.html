• Drake

    Scorpion is the fifth studio album by Canadian rapper Drake. It was released on June 29, 2018 by Young Money Entertainment, Cash Money Records and Republic Records.Scorpion is a double album consisting of 25 tracks. Its A-side is primarily hip hop, whilst its B-side has been described as R&B and pop.

  • Eminem

    Kamikaze (stylized as KAMIKAZÆŽ) is the tenth studio album by American rapper Eminem.[2] Previously unannounced, the album was released on August 31, 2018 by Aftermath Entertainment, Interscope Records, and Shady Records.[3] The album features guest appearances from Joyner Lucas, Royce da 5'9", Jessie Reyez, and uncredited vocals by Justin Vernon. Eminem, credited as Slim Shady, and Dr. Dre served as executive producers, while production for individual tracks comes from a variety of musicians. The album has generated some controversy for use of homophobic slurs and diss tracks. It has topped the charts in Australia, Finland, Ireland, Italy, the Netherlands, New Zealand, Norway, Scotland, Sweden, the United Kingdom, the United States.
